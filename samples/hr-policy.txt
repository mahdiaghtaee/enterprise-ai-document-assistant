Human Resources Policy

Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to two days per week with manager approval. Remote work requests should be submitted at least three business days before the planned remote work date.

Leave Requests
Annual leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal. Managers should review leave requests within five business days.

Equipment
Company laptops and accessories must be returned when employment ends. Lost or damaged equipment must be reported to IT within one business day.

Confidentiality
Employees must not store confidential customer, financial, or employee data on personal devices.
