Contract Review Policy

Vendor Contracts
Vendor contracts must be reviewed by Operations and Finance before approval. Contracts with a value above 50,000 USD must also be reviewed by Legal.

Approval Timeline
Standard vendor contracts should be reviewed within seven business days. Urgent requests require written justification from the requesting department.

Signatures
Only authorized signatories may sign contracts on behalf of the company. Signed contracts must be stored in the central document system.

Renewals
Contract renewals should be reviewed at least thirty days before expiration.
